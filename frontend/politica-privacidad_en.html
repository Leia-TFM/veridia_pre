Section 1: Data privacy legislation and your digital rights

What is this Policy about?

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. This document explains how we process information generated when you use our advertising platform developed within the Master’s in Artificial Intelligence (2025–2026 academic year) at the Autonomous University of Madrid.

This platform is academic and research-based. It allows publishing and viewing advertisements verified using artificial intelligence techniques. Access to the platform is provided without prior registration, so we do not directly collect identifying personal data.

This Policy also informs you about your privacy rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union and Organic Law 3/2018 on Personal Data Protection and guarantee of digital rights (LOPDGDD).

Who is responsible?

The formal responsibility of this platform lies with the Autonomous University of Madrid (UAM), located at:

Autonomous University of Madrid
Cantoblanco Campus
28049 Madrid, Spain

Project developed by students of the Master in Artificial Intelligence, 2025–2026.

The UAM acts as an academic supervising institution. However, the University is not responsible for the truthfulness, security, or accuracy of the advertisements published on the platform. Ad verification is purely indicative and does not constitute institutional endorsement.

Applicable legal framework

  • Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) — General Data Protection Regulation.
  • Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD) — Personal Data Protection and guarantee of digital rights in Spain.
  • Law 34/2002 (LSSI-CE) — Information Society Services and Electronic Commerce Act.

Your privacy rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding any data we may process:

  • Access: You may request information about data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure (“right to be forgotten”): You may request deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary.
  • Restriction: You may request limited processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You may object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Complaint: You may file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).

Section 2: Personal data

What personal data do we collect?

No registration required

Our platform is designed under the principle of privacy by default: users can access and interact with ads without creating an account or providing personal data.

We do not request or store:

  • First name, last name, or nickname.
  • Email address.
  • Phone number.
  • Identity documents.
  • Payment information.

The login system, when available, uses token-based or anonymous session authentication, without linking activity to a direct personal identity.

Types of data sent for analysis

Users submit content for verification. There are two input types, both processed anonymously:

Input type Description Purpose
Type 1 — Written ad The user directly enters the advertisement text to be verified. AI-based analysis and indicative verification
Type 2 — Ad URL The user provides a URL link to the ad or page to be verified. The platform accesses publicly available content from that URL. AI-based analysis and indicative verification

Section 3: How we use your data

Purposes of processing

The collected technical data is used exclusively for:

  • Platform operation: Ensuring availability and proper functioning of the service. This includes processing user queries correctly and returning a verification result. This refers to technical operation, not the truthfulness of ad content.
  • Indicative ad verification: Applying AI models to analyze ad content (text or URL). The result is a probability indicator, not a certification of authenticity.
  • System improvement: Aggregated and anonymous analysis to improve AI models within the academic project.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent or malicious use.

Important: The platform guarantees analysis service, but does not guarantee or certify the truthfulness, accuracy, or legitimacy of submitted ads. Results are for guidance only.

We do not use data for commercial purposes, nor share it with third parties for advertising.

International data transfers

No data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA). All infrastructure operates within UAM academic environments or EU-based servers.

Section 4: Ads and data retention

Nature of verified ads

The platform uses artificial intelligence techniques to analyze and classify published ads. However, this verification is purely indicative:

  • It does not guarantee content accuracy.
  • It does not certify the advertiser’s identity.
  • It does not constitute institutional endorsement by UAM.
  • AI models may produce errors or inherit biases from training data.

Users must exercise their own judgment before acting on any ad published on the platform.

Section 5: Security and responsibility

UAM liability limitations

The Autonomous University of Madrid is not responsible for the security, accuracy, or suitability of ads published on the platform, nor for any damages resulting from interaction with such ads. This platform is an experimental academic project and should be understood as such.

The UAM supervises the project from an academic and research perspective, but cannot guarantee the absence of errors, service interruptions, or security breaches. In case of a security incident affecting personal data, we will notify the AEPD within the timeframe required by GDPR (72 hours).

Organic Law on Personal Data Protection and guarantee of digital rights

1. Purpose and data controller

This Privacy Policy regulates the processing of data generated during the use of the academic advertising verification platform based on artificial intelligence, developed within the Master in Artificial Intelligence (2025–2026) at the Autonomous University of Madrid (UAM).

2. Applicable legal framework

The data processing carried out within this platform is governed by the following regulations:

  • Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).
  • Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD).
  • Law 34/2002 (LSSI-CE).

3. Personal data collected

The platform is designed under the principle of privacy by default (Article 25 GDPR). No registration is required, and no identifying data such as names, emails, phone numbers, identity documents, or payment information is collected or stored.

5. Purpose of data processing

The technical data collected is used exclusively for:

  • Ensuring service availability and proper technical functioning.
  • Applying AI models to provide indicative verification of ad content.
  • Improving models through aggregated and anonymous analysis in an academic research context.
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent or misuse activities.
  • Collecting linguistic information to provide statistical insights for NGOs and users to identify patterns and help reduce fraudulent ads.
  • Using the data to support scalability and future improvements of the system.

No data is used for commercial purposes or shared with third parties for advertising. No international transfers outside the EEA are performed.

This is covered by Intellectual Property Law, Article 32 on citations and use for educational or scientific research purposes, allowing non-commercial academic use.

6. User rights

In accordance with Chapter III of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, users may exercise the following rights:

  • Access right: obtain confirmation of whether personal data is being processed and access it.
  • Rectification right: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure right: request deletion of data when no longer necessary.
  • Restriction right: request suspension of processing under Article 18 GDPR.
  • Objection right: object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Portability right: receive data in a structured, commonly used format.
